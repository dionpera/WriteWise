Linking Words Dictionary

Moreover: Used to add further information or to emphasize a point.

Example: "The project requires a lot of research. Moreover, it demands creativity and time management."

Therefore: Indicates a result or consequence.

Example: "She studied hard for the exam. Therefore, she passed with excellent marks."

However: Used to introduce a contrast or contradiction.

Example: "I wanted to go to the park. However, it started to rain."

Additionally: Used to add more information.

Example: "The company offers excellent benefits. Additionally, the work environment is fantastic."

Consequently: Indicates a result or outcome.

Example: "She didn't submit the report on time. Consequently, her promotion was delayed."
